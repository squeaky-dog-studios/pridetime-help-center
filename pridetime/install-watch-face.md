# How to Install PrideTime on Your Watch

After installing PrideTime from the Google Play Store, there's one more step on your
**watch itself** to make it your active watch face. It only takes a few seconds, but the
watch will ask for a permission along the way — here's exactly what to expect.

![Annotated setup flow showing Step 1 (tap PrideTime), Step 2 (tap Allow), Step 3 (Setting up PrideTime), and Step 4 (PrideTime is ready, then swipe to dismiss)]({{ 'pridetime/images/install-watch-face-instructions.jpg' | relative_url }})

## Step 1: Open PrideTime from your watch's app list

On your watch, open the app list and tap the **PrideTime** icon.

## Step 2: Allow PrideTime to change your watch face

Your watch will ask:

> **Allow PrideTime to change the active watch face?**

Tap **Allow**.

This permission is required — it's how PrideTime is able to set itself as your watch
face. Without it, PrideTime can't complete setup.

## Step 3: Wait a moment while it sets up

You'll briefly see a **"Setting up PrideTime..."** screen. This usually only takes a
second or two.

## Step 4: You're done!

Once you see **"PrideTime is ready!"**, your watch face has been set successfully.

Swipe to dismiss this screen, and you'll see PrideTime as your active watch face.

## Troubleshooting

**I tapped "Don't allow" by accident.**
No problem — just reopen the PrideTime app from your watch's app list and try again
from Step 2.

**Nothing happened after I tapped Allow.**
Give it a few seconds — on some watches the setup screen can take a little longer to
appear. If it still doesn't show up after 30 seconds or so, try closing the PrideTime
app on your watch completely and opening it again.

**Still stuck?**
[Contact support]({{ '/pridetime/' | relative_url }}) and we'll help you sort it out.
